# Audio credits

- **SFX packs `chime`, `wood`, `pop`, `bubble`, `crystal`, `arcade`** and **all
  music tracks** are procedurally generated by `apps/web/scripts/generate-*.mjs`
  (no external assets).
- **SFX pack `clean`** uses sounds from Kenney's *Interface Sounds* pack
  (https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds), released under **CC0 1.0**
  (public domain — no attribution required). Files were renamed to the cue names.
  These are NOT regenerated by the script; they are committed static assets.
